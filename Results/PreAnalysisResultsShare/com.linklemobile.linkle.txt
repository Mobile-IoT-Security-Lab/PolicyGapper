5. SHARING OF PERSONAL DATA
With Other Users
You share information with other users when you voluntarily disclose information on the service (including your public
profile). Please be careful with your information and make sure that the content you share is stuff that you're
comfortable being publically viewable since neither you nor we can control what others do with your information once
you share it. If you choose to limit the audience for all or part of your profile or for certain content or information about
you, then it will be visible according to your settings.

With Our Service Providers And Partners
We share your information to others, who help us provide and improve our Products (e.g., maintenance, analysis, audit,
payments, fraud detection, marketing, and development). Service providers will have access to your information as

purposes. We use processors such as Appsflyer, Google, Facebook, Braze, Amazon Web Services, Inc., or Zendesk, Inc.
