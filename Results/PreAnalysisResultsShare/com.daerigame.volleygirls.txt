III. Sharing and Provision to Third Parties
We do not provide personal information to third parties beyond the stated purposes without consent or
a legal basis. Exceptions include:
1. Where necessary for billing and settlement
2. For statistics, academic research, or market research in de-identified/aggregated form
3. Upon lawful requests from investigative authorities
4. Where required by special laws (e.g., Real Name Financial Transactions Act,
Telecommunications Business Act)
When partnering or outsourcing, we will disclose the recipient, purpose, and items provided in advance
and obtain consent where required.

---
IV. Outsourcing of Processing
We may entrust personal information processing to service providers as necessary and require
appropriate safeguards by contract in accordance with applicable law.
