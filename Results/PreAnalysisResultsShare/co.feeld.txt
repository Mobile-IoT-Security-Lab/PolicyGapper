As part of our ongoing work to make your enjoyment of the Services as safe as possible, we will also collect and process
your telephonic data, specifically the telephone number you provide at account registration and creation, to facilitate
two-step SMS authentication of your account. This SMS authentication involves the sharing of your telephone number

prevent the creation of fake or robotic Member accounts. The Member-provided telephone numbers will be retained by
the third-party for up to 400 days.

Age Estimation and Identity Verification Notice
As part of our efforts to provide a safe and protected Feeld experience, Feeld will collect and process your facial
biometric data and cellular mobile network operator data through a trusted third party (Yoti) in conjunction with our other
Customer Support applications and platforms. Age verification provided by Yoti will be implemented to (a) ensure that
everyone using our platform is 18 years old or older; and (b) help prevent any fraudulent or malicious actors from
accessing Feeld services. In the event of support ticket escalations, Members may elect to provide a valid government
identification card for age verification purposes. If a Member elects to provide government ID data, said data will only
be processed for the limited purpose of verifying the Member's age and shall be purged from storage immediately after
verification is complete. You can find Yoti's Biometric Policy as linked herein.

---
Session Replays (Amplitude)
Feeld may collect data relating to your navigation and use of the Feeld app via third-party service provider Amplitude.
This collection of data may include the recording of your screen activity while you use and navigate the Feeld app
("Session Replay"). The purpose of this data collection is strictly for measurement of operational quality, bug tracking,
and beta testing geared towards optimising the performance of the Feeld platform. Accordingly, Member-created
content, such as photographs, private chats, and account and profile information shall be masked and unviewable
through any and all Session Replays. All data collected through a Session Replay shall be deleted after thirty (30) days.
