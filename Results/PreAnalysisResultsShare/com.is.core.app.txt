1. Introduction
1.1. Comera is an end-to-end encrypted internet calling and messaging application that offers text messaging, voice and
video calling, group chats, and media sharing services, as well as an integrated platform called Comera Souq for
accessing third-party services. This Privacy Policy explains how Comera Technology L.L.C. ("Comera", "we", "us" or "the
Company") collects, uses, shares, and protects your personal data when you use the Comera mobile app and the
Comera Souq platform (collectively, our "Services"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and handling your
personal data in compliance with all applicable laws, including the UAE Federal Decree-Law No. 45 of 2021 on Personal
Data Protection (PDPL), and the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR). By using our Services, you agree to the
collection and use of your information as described in this Privacy Policy and our Terms of Use.

---

1.2. We act as the "data controller" of your personal data when providing the Services (meaning we determine the
purposes and means of processing). For any third-party services accessed via Comera Souq, the respective third-party
providers are independent controllers of any personal data you provide to them, and their own privacy policies will apply.
We encourage you to review those third-party privacy policies when using Comera Souq features.

---

4. Comera Souq and Third-Party Services
4.1. Comera Souq is a feature within the Comera app that allows you to access a variety of services offered by
independent third-party providers. These services are integrated via secure iframes or APIs to provide a seamless in-app
experience. When you choose to use a third-party service through Comera Souq:

---

4.1.1. Data Collection by Third Parties: You may be asked to provide information directly to the third-party service
provider. Such information is collected by the third-party within the embedded iframe or via the API integration, and is
subject to that provider's own privacy policy. Comera does not receive the content of forms you fill in an embedded
third-party page, unless it is necessary for the integration to function.

---

4.1.2. Data Shared by Comera: To facilitate the integration, we might share limited data with the third-party service. For
instance, if a Comera Souq game or service needs to identify your Comera account, we may confirm your user ID or
phone number, or if you choose to use an Al chatbot, we may transmit your query to the Al service provider to get a
response. We will only share the minimum data necessary for the service to operate, and only with your initiation. No
third-party service receives your Comera messages or contacts from us, unless you actively invoke that service to do
SO.

---

4.1.3. Independent Responsibility: Third-party providers accessible through Comera Souq are independent controllers
of the data they collect. Comera is not responsible for how third-party providers handle your information, and their
actions are not governed by this Privacy Policy. However, if you encounter an issue with a third-party service integrated
in our app, we would appreciate feedback and will endeavor to ensure the security of integrations. We strongly
recommend you review the privacy policy of any third-party service before proceeding.
