Sharing personal data with third parties
AAWireless does not sell your data to third parties and only provides your data to third parties if
this is required for the execution of the agreement with you or to comply with a legal obligation.
AAWireless enters into a data processing agreement with companies that process your data on
behalf of AAWireless to ensure the same level of security and confidentiality of your data. Mr
Chiel Prins is responsible for processing data within AAWireless.
