4. Sharing Photos with Third Parties
No Face Data Shared:
GIGI does not share face data with third parties. However, user-uploaded photos are sent to third-party
APIs to apply the virtual outfits and visualizations. These third-party services process the images
temporarily to create the visualizations and return the processed data (outfit visualization) to GIGI.

---

5. Reasons for Sharing Photos with Third Parties
We share the uploaded photos with third-party APIs for the sole purpose of applying virtual outfits and
visualizations to the user's image. These third-party services are essential for processing the photos and
providing the virtual try-on experience.

---

6. Third-Party Storage of Photos
Do Third Parties Store Photos?
The third-party services we use do not retain the photos after processing. Once the outfit visualization
is applied, the data is returned to GIGI, and the photos are deleted from the third-party servers. No
