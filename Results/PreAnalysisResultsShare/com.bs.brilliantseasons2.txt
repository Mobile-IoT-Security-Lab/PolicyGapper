Information collection and Use

For a better experience, the app may request access to certain device permissions
(such as camera or storage) to function properly, but it does not collect or store
personal information. However, the app may use third-party services that may
collect personal information as part of their operations. These third-party services
may include:
•
Google Play Services
•
Google Analytics for Firebase
•
Firebase Crashlytics by Google
These services may collect non-personally identifiable data, including Log Data,
such as device name, operating system version, IP address, app configuration
details, and usage statistics. Additionally, depending on their terms, they may also
collect personal information. This data is used solely for monitoring app
performance, identifying causes for app crashes, and improving the app's
functionality. The legal basis for this processing is legitimate interest in maintaining
and enhancing app performance. We encourage users to review the privacy policies
of these third-party services to understand how they handle data.

---

User Rights under GDPR
Since we do not directly collect or store personal information, we do not process
user data. However, users have rights under the General Data Protection Regulation
(GDPR) concerning data collected by third-party services, including:
• The right to access, modify, or delete any personal data collected by third-
party services.
• The right to object to data processing, where applicable.
• The right to withdraw consent (if applicable) for third-party services that rely
on consent-based processing.
• The right to request data portability from relevant third-party providers.
To exercise these rights, users should refer to the respective privacy policies of
third-party services used by the app and follow their instructions for managing
data.

---

Retention of information
As the developer does not directly handle any personal information, there is no
storage or retention of personally identifiable data by us. However, third-party
service providers may retain usage data, including personal information, for
analytical and operational purposes, as outlined in their respective privacy policies.

---

Opting Out of Data Collection
Users who wish to limit data collection by third-party services may:
• Adjust their device settings to limit app permissions.
•
Disable interest-based ads via their Google or Apple account settings.
•
Use Google Play or Apple App Store privacy settings to manage third-party
data sharing.

---

Transfer of information
Since the app does not collect or store personal information directly, there is no
transfer of such data by us. However, analytics and crash reports processed by
third-party services may involve data transfers in accordance with their privacy
policies and applicable data protection laws.

---

Privacy of children
The app does not knowingly collect any personal information from children under
the age of 13. Since no user accounts are created and no personal data is stored,
there is no risk of unauthorized data collection from minors. If you believe that a
child has provided personal information to a third-party service used by the app,
please contact the respective service provider to request deletion.

---

Links to other resources
The Mobile Application and Services may contain links to external resources that
are not owned or controlled by us. We are not responsible for the privacy practices
of third parties and encourage users to review their privacy policies before
interacting with their services.

---

Information security
We strive to maintain high security standards to protect non-personally identifiable
data processed by third-party services. However, no digital system is entirely
secure, and we cannot guarantee the absolute security of transmitted data.

---

Data breach
As we do not collect or store personal information, there is no risk of a personal
data breach from our direct handling of user data. In the event of a security
incident affecting third-party services, users will be notified by those service
providers in accordance with their privacy policies and applicable laws.
