We will never sell any of your data to anyone, and we
require partners and third parties to follow the rules that

disclosing the data we provide.
Specifically, we share data with the following third parties:
(a) We work with third-party partners who help us improve
Buenovela, which makes it possible to operate our
companies and provide services to people around the world.
(b) We do not provide any personally identifiable data
(c) to these third-party ad servers or ad networks without
your consent or except as part of a specific program or
feature for which you will have the ability to opt-in or opt-
out.
(d) Members, subsidiaries, or affiliates of our corporate
group, make Buenovela improving and optimizing.
(e) Law enforcement agencies, public or tax authorities or
other organizations if legally required to do so.
