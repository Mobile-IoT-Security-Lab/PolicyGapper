Advertising Data

Like many apps, our App is in part supported by advertising. Unless you opt-out, discussed more below,
we may serve ads to you by transmitting to our select advertising partners a standard advertising
identifier (an “Advertiser ID”) that we collect from your device. Advertising IDs are standard across the
mobile advertising industry and are used to identify a particular device for advertising purposes.
Advertising IDs are random identifiers that are generated by and tied to your mobile device. An example
from a Google Android device looks like this: cdda802e-fb9c-46af-9846-0233d394c712.

---

Advertising IDs are typically generated once per device, so your device would always be associated with
the same Advertising ID. Advertising IDs don't contain any Personal Information in and of themselves.
However, because they are typically static, a device will maintain the same Advertising ID over time, it is
possible for third parties to use an Advertising ID in conjunction with location data they could collect
from your device or from other apps you use, and other apps known to be associated with your device's
Advertising ID, to create inferences about you. Both Apple's iOS or Google's Android operating system
provide a mechanism for users to reset or disable their device's Advertising ID. If you elect to disable
your Advertising ID, we respect that choice and will not send your device's Advertising ID to our
advertising partners.

---

Our Period Calendar applications transmit a device's Advertising ID without adding any other Personal
Information or other additional information. We believe this results in an excellent user experience with
relevant and helpful advertising, while also protecting your privacy and safety.

mechanisms we provide for you to control certain uses and disclosures of your information.
