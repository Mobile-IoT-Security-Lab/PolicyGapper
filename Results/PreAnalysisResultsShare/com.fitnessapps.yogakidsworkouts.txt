Our Privacy Policy describes the information collected by our company GunjanApps Studios, and our partners, through our
mobile applications and the use of the information.

---

Compliance with COPPA & GDPR
We follow the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) in the U.S., which requires us to inform parents or legal
guardians about how we collect, use, and share personal data from children under 13 years old. Before allowing children
under 13 to access certain features, we obtain parental consent.

--- [PAGE BREAK] ---

Sharing Information
We will not share a child's personal information with other individuals or companies except in limited situations. When other
companies perform services for us, like customer support, email services, delivery services, call centers, data management
services, help desk providers, law firms, payment processors, accountants and shopping cart providers; however, these
companies are prohibited from using a child's personal information for purposes other than those requested by us or
required by law

---

The list of third parties that have access to the personal data collected by us is available in our Privacy Policy.

Right to Know About Data Sharing:
We may share limited personal data with third-party service providers for essential functions like customer support,
analytics, and advertising. However, we do not sell user's personal data. Users have the right to request a full list of third
parties who have access to their personal data by contacting us at support@gunjanappstudios.com.

---

Advertising Practices
Our apps designed for children do not serve personalized advertisements or ineterst-based Ads. We collaborate with trusted
ad partners (such as [AdMob, Unity Ads, etc.]) that offer contextual ads only, that support COPPA, GDPR-K, and Apple's Kids
Category policies. All advertisements displayed in our IOS kids category apps are subject to manual review to ensure they
are appropriate for young audiences, free from objectionable or sensitive content, and compliant with COPPA and Apple's
Kids Category guidelines. We regularly review our ad practices to maintain full compliance with Apple's Kids Category
policies, as well as regional privacy regulations.

Analytics Data and Information Collected
When you or your children use our apps and services, certain information may be collected automatically. This helps us
improve our apps and make them more engaging for kids and other users. We also use this data for internal purposes, such as
enhancing our services. We might use Firebase (https://firebase.google.com), a third-party analytics service provided by
Google. Firebase automatically collects the following technical and non-personal data:
• IP address;
• Number of sessions;
•
Session duration;
• Retention metrics;
• Events triggered during app usage;
•
Install source; and
•
IDFA (Identifier for Advertisers).
How we use collected information:
• Analyze how users interact with the apps;
• Optimize performance and improve user experience;
• Detect and prevent fraud or misuse of the apps;
•
Maintain app security; and
• Deliver relevant advertisements.
We might retain this data for as long as necessary to fulfill these purposes, but no longer than one year. The legal basis for
processing this data is our legitimate business interest (to improve, protect, and grow our apps) and your consent where
applicable.
