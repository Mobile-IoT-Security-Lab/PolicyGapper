This Privacy Policy details how your personal information is collected, processed,
used, and shared by Smart media internet marketing Itd. (referred to as "Walli”,
"the Company”, “we”, “us”, or “our”) when you use the Walli mobile application (the
"Application"). Smart media internet marketing ltd. is the legal entity acting as the
data controller for the personal information collected and processed through Walli.

---
1. What Information Does the
Application Obtain and How Is It
Used?

---
The Application obtains information you provide and collects certain information
automatically.

---
a) User Provided Information

The Application obtains the information you provide when you download, register,
and use the Application. This may include:

• When you register with us and use the Application: your full name, email
address, username, password, website, links to your social networks
(Instagram, Facebook, Twitter, etc.), and your biography.
• Transaction-related information: such as when you respond to any offers, or
download or use applications from us (Note: If you make purchases, payment
information is typically processed by the app store provider, e.g., Google Play
or Apple App Store, subject to their privacy policies).
• Information you provide when you contact us for help.
• Information you enter into our system when using the Application: such as
likes, downloads, artists you follow, and content you interact with.

---
b) Automatically Collected Information

In addition, the Application may collect the following information automatically:

• Device Information: The type of mobile device you use, your mobile device's
unique device ID (or other identifiers), IP address, mobile operating system,
device model, and manufacturer.
• Advertising Identifier: Your mobile device's Advertising ID (e.g., IDFA for iOS,
GAID for Android).
• Network Information: The type of mobile Internet browsers you use, the name
of your mobile operator or ISP, network connection type (Wi-Fi, cellular), and
network status.
• Usage Information: Information about the way you use the Application, such
as features used, content viewed, wallpapers applied, time spent in the app,
interactions with advertisements, language, and time zone settings.

---
• Location Information (if enabled): When you use the Application, we may use
GPS technology (or other similar technology) to determine your current
location (e.g., city level) to personalize content, display relevant
advertisements, and improve our services. You can control location access via
your device settings or within the Application settings (see Section 8 below).
• Crash Logs and Diagnostics: Information related to crashes and performance
issues, including device state information, unique device identifiers, and
location information, to help diagnose and fix problems. This data is often
processed via third-party services like Firebase Crashlytics.
• Other Information from Permissions: Depending on the permissions you
grant, the app may access features like storage (to save wallpapers), network
state, ability to set wallpapers, receive notifications, etc., solely to enable
Application functionality. We collect information related to the use of these
permissions to operate and improve the Application.

---
We use this automatically collected information to understand user behavior,
personalize the content (including ads) and features you see, improve the
Application's performance and stability, provide relevant services, and for security
purposes.

---
c) Access to Third-Party Service Accounts

The Application may allow you to connect your account with third-party services
like Facebook or Google for login or feature integration. These services require
explicit authorization from you.

• Access to Facebook account (Meta Platforms, Inc.): Allows connection with
your Facebook account. Permissions asked: Email and Public Profile. Place of
processing: US (and potentially other locations as per Meta's policy).
• Access to Google account (Google LLC): Allows connection with your Google
account. Permissions asked: Email and Profile ID. Place of processing: US
(and potentially other locations as per Google's policy).

---
• Right to Know (Specific to certain jurisdictions like California): The right to

You have several options to control or limit the collection and use of your
