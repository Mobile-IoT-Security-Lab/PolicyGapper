[SECTION: User Rights under GDPR]
---
Since we do not directly collect or store personal information, we do not process
user data. However, users have rights under the General Data Protection Regulation
(GDPR) concerning data collected by third-party services, including:
• The right to access, modify, or delete any personal data collected by third-
party services.
• The right to object to data processing, where applicable.
• The right to withdraw consent (if applicable) for third-party services that rely
on consent-based processing.
• The right to request data portability from relevant third-party providers.
---
[SECTION: Retention of information]
---
As the developer does not directly handle any personal information, there is no
storage or retention of personally identifiable data by us. However, third-party
service providers may retain usage data, including personal information, for
analytical and operational purposes, as outlined in their respective privacy policies.

[SECTION: Opting Out of Data Collection]
---
Users who wish to limit data collection by third-party services may:
• Adjust their device settings to limit app permissions.
•
Disable interest-based ads via their Google or Apple account settings.
•
Use Google Play or Apple App Store privacy settings to manage third-party
data sharing.
