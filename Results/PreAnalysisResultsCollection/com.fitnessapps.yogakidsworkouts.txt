Compliance with COPPA & GDPR
We follow the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) in the U.S., which requires us to inform parents or legal
guardians about how we collect, use, and share personal data from children under 13 years old. Before allowing children
under 13 to access certain features, we obtain parental consent.

---

Similarly, the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) in some European Union countries requires us to inform parents
about data collection for children under 16 years old and obtain their consent before granting access to certain features.

---

Parental Consent
If we need to collect personal data from a child under 13 (U.S.) or under 16 (EU), we will first seek verifiable parental consent.
Parents can read what data is collected from our "Personal Data Collected through our apps section" along with how the
data will be used and how to withdraw consent.

---

Stopping the Collection or Use of a Child's Personal Information:
Parents can withdraw their consent at any time to stop the collection or processing of their child's data. To do so, email
support@gunjanappstudios.com with your request. Upon receiving a valid request, we will disable data collection for the
child and delete existing data where applicable.
