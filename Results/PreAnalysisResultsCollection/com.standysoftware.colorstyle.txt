Data Collection and Use
While using our Service we collect and share with third party service providers certain data from
our users, including but not limited to IP address (approximate location), device and advertising

This data is anonymous and encrypted in transit. It is required for analytics, advertising and
support purposes.

Image Processing
The app uses a third party service (Firebase Machine Learning Kit) to do face features detection
from images provided by you. This process can be conducted locally on your device and/or on third
party servers (online). During online image recognition your images are stored in the memory only
and deleted after the end of image processing. For more information about data usage during
online image recognition, please, review the following document.
