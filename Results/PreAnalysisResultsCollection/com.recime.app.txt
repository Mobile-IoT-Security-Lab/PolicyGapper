Tracking Technologies and Cookies
Tracking technologies used are beacons, tags, and scripts to collect and track information
and to improve and analyze the Service. The technologies we use may include:
• Cookies or Browser Cookies. You can instruct your browser to refuse all Cookies or to
indicate when a Cookie is being sent. However, if you do not accept Cookies, you may not
be able to use some parts of the Service. Unless you have adjusted your browser setting so
that it will refuse Cookies, the Service may use Cookies.
• Web Beacons. Certain sections of the Service and our emails may contain small electronic
files known as web beacons (also referred to as clear gifs, pixel tags, and single-pixel gifs)
that permit us to count users who have visited those pages or opened an email and for
other related website statistics (for example, recording the popularity of a certain section
and verifying system and server integrity).
