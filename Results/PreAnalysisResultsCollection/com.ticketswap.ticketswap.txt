Why can we process your personal data?
We process your personal data on the following legal bases:
• Consent: For sending newsletters and direct marketing through email, push notifications
and marketing via other channels we request your permission. This permission can also
--- [PAGE BREAK] ---
easily be withdrawn through the unsubscribe link in each newsletter or by going to your
account settings and disabling the toggle for email preferences.
• Performance of the agreement: We process your general information, payment
information, social information and electronic identifiers to provide our Services to you as a
user.
• Legal obligation: We are required to store certain general information and payment data in
our records for Tax Authorities, such as invoice data, bank account details, phone number
and your country of residence.
• Legitimate interest: For processing general information, social information and electronic
identifiers, in order to log user activities to identify and prevent unlawful use of the account
and Services. As part of this, we may use your personal data for purposes of A/B testing to
improve our services and user experience. To fulfill our tax obligations, we process various
(personal) data to determine where a transaction took place (and consequently, VAT is
due), namely the country of residence provided by you, your IP-address, the prefix of your
phone number, the country code of your IBAN and the country of the event.Further, we
share your name and e-mail address with event organizers to allow them to send
information about cancellation, change of an event and health and safety during the event.
In case you have purchased an e-ticket through SecureSwap services provided by the
event organizer, we may share your email address with the organizer of the event to allow
the organizer to update you on new events.
--- [PAGE BREAK] ---
Depending on your (optional) consent, we may use the content of any user reviews you
submit to our website for marketing purposes on our website and third party websites. You
may object to this processing at any time by sending an email to privacy@ticketswap.com.

Social Features
TicketSwap offers social features to make your experience on our platform more personal
and interactive. When you use these social features and add the following information, we
process the following personal data based on your consent:
• Profile Information: You may choose to provide profile information such as your username,
bio, profile picture, and your favorite artists or events you are following.
• Following and Followers: You can follow other users or be followed by them. You can
control whether your profile is public or only visible to approved followers.
• Visibility of Activities: Depending on your privacy settings, other users may see which
artists and events you follow.
We use this data to:
• Display your profile on the platform to other users in social features, according to your
privacy preferences.
• Enable you to make social connections with other users.
• Improve our recommendations based on the artists and events you follow.
--- [PAGE BREAK] ---
We use the services of “Processors” for the processing of (personal) data. Within this context
these Processors receive personal data from us which they process on our behalf and for
our purposes. We use, for instance, processors for hosting our data, assisting us in support
services, providing (marketing and platform) analytics and insights and sending out mass
communication (e.g. via mail and SMS). One example of a third-party processor that we use is
OpenAl. We use OpenAl for extracting information from tickets that are uploaded by our
users. The output from OpenAl will help our users with automatically filling in details about a
ticket including event title, ticket category and ticket price.
