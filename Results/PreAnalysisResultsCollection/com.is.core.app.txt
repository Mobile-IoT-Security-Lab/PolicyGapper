1. Introduction
1.1. Comera is an end-to-end encrypted internet calling and messaging application that offers text messaging, voice and
video calling, group chats, and media sharing services, as well as an integrated platform called Comera Souq for
accessing third-party services. This Privacy Policy explains how Comera Technology L.L.C. ("Comera", "we", "us" or "the
Company") collects, uses, shares, and protects your personal data when you use the Comera mobile app and the
Comera Souq platform (collectively, our "Services"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and handling your
personal data in compliance with all applicable laws, including the UAE Federal Decree-Law No. 45 of 2021 on Personal
Data Protection (PDPL), and the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR). By using our Services, you agree to the
collection and use of your information as described in this Privacy Policy and our Terms of Use.
---

--- [SECTION: 3. Information We Collect] ---
We collect various types of information from and about you when you use Comera, including:

3.1. Information You Provide: You may provide personal data directly to us:

3.1.1. Account Information: When creating a Comera account, you provide your mobile phone number and a profile
name or nickname. You may also add an optional profile photo and status message to your profile.
---
3.1.2. Contacts: If you grant the app access to your phone's contact list, we collect phone numbers and names from
your address book to help you connect with friends on Comera. We use this information to show you which of your
contacts are using Comera. Contacts who are not yet on Comera are hashed or processed in a way that prevents us
from identifying them. You can always choose not to grant contact access; however, certain social features (like
identifying Comera users in your contacts) will be limited.
---
3.1.4. User Communications: When you contact Comera for support or otherwise communicate with us, you may
provide us with your name, email address, and the content of your inquiry. We will retain such correspondence and
information as needed to respond and resolve your issue, consistent with this Policy.
---
3.1.5. Optional Features: You may choose to provide additional information when using certain features. If you share
your location in a chat, we will collect and transmit your geolocation to your chosen contacts with your permission; we
do not track your location in the background or use your location for any other purpose without consent.
---
3.1.6. Information Collected Automatically: When you use Comera, we automatically collect certain technical and
usage data:
---
3.1.6.1. Usage and Log Data: We gather diagnostic and performance information such as log files, crash reports, and
usage logs. This helps us troubleshoot issues and improve our Services.
---
3.1.6.2. Device and Connection Information: We collect data about your mobile device and network when you install or
use Comera. This includes device model, operating system version, hardware identifiers, app version, browser type (for
web access), battery level, mobile network or ISP, regional and language settings, time zone, and IP address. We may
also collect your device's unique identifiers (such as device ID or SIM IMSI) as needed for account security and to
prevent unauthorized access.
---
3.1.6.3. Cookies and Similar Technologies: Like most online services, we use cookies and similar tracking
technologies in our app and website to store preferences and gather analytics data. For example, cookies help us keep
you logged in on our web portal and remember your choices. We also use cookies or mobile SDKs to analyze how
users interact with our Services (e.g., which features are most used) and to customize your experience. You can
--- [PAGE BREAK] ---
manage cookie preferences via our website's cookie settings or your browser settings. For mobile app analytics, you
can reset your advertising identifier or opt out of personalized ads in your device settings. For more information,
please see our Cookie Policy.
---
3.1.7. Information from Third-Party Sources: We may receive information about you from other users or external
sources:
---
3.1.7.1. Your Contacts on Comera: Other Comera users may provide your phone number and name (for example, by
syncing their address book). They may also send you messages or call you. We require that users only share
information they have the right to share.
---
3.1.7.2. Third-Party Login or Import: If we enable third-party account integration (for example, logging in via a social
media account or importing a profile picture from another service), we will obtain information from that third party with
your permission, such as your profile name and avatar from that account.
---
3.1.7.3. User Reports: If another user or a third party reports you for violating our Terms (e.g., for spam or abuse), we
may receive information as part of that report, including the reporter's user ID and the content of the report. We will
only use this information to review and address the issue in accordance with our policies.

--- [SECTION: 10. User Controls and Account Management] ---
10.3. Managing Contacts: You can add or remove contacts from Comera. If you have synced your phone contacts and
later wish to revoke that permission, you can do so in your device settings; this will stop further sync and you can
request we delete any contacts data stored (though normally we store non-user contacts in a hashed form). You can
also mark certain contacts as 'Permanent' within Comera to back them up for easy recovery on a new device, or remove
such designations at any time.
---
10.4. Notifications and Preferences: Through your device or app settings, you can control Comera notifications (for
example, disabling message previews, or muting notifications for certain chats). You may also opt out of certain data
collection: for instance, you can disable location sharing permissions if you do not want to use that feature, or decline
optional diagnostic data collection if prompted.

--- [SECTION: 12. Children's Privacy] ---
12.1. Our Services are not directed to children, and we do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If you are
minor as per the applicable laws, then you are not permitted to use Comera. If we learn that we have inadvertently
collected personal information from a child below the applicable age, we will promptly delete such information. Parents
or guardians should supervise their children's use of online services and can request us to remove any information
inadvertently collected from their minor children by contacting us at our privacy contact email.
---
12.2. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child under the age of consent has provided personal data to
Comera without your consent, please contact us immediately. We will take steps to delete the data as soon as possible.
