Extra information for IRMO - Al Video Photo Generator
Data Storage and Protection:
•
Purpose of Uploads:
Users upload images solely to generate Al-powered visual results, such as virtual try-ons, Al portraits, Al

videos, or similar effects. The app does not employ facial recognition technology for user identification or
verification.
---
• Use of Face Data:
Face data (i.e., the uploaded images containing faces) is used exclusively to:
•
•
Generate Al-modified visuals such as virtual try-on previews, Al portraits, Al videos, or
personalized image content.
•
Train a one-time Al model for the user's requested feature (e.g., image transformation, video
creation).
•
Display and return processed results to the user within the app interface.
---
• Restrictions on Face Data Usage:
The collected face data is never used for user identification, biometric analysis, surveillance, or
advertising personalization.
---
• Data Storage Location:
Data is stored temporarily on secure servers managed by IRMO and trusted cloud providers located in
compliant regions (e.g., EU or US). Images are not accessible to any external users or parties beyond the
required third-party processing pipeline.
---
• Data Retention and User Control:
Face data is retained until the user chooses to delete it. This is because the app interface allows users to
view both the original uploaded images and the Al-generated results at any time.
•
User Control: Images (both original and generated) are stored securely and remain available in the
app until manually deleted by the user.
•
Deletion Request: If a user deletes a photo via the app interface, it is deleted from IRMO servers
and third-party caches within 30 days.
•
Automatic Deletion (if incomplete): Temporary files from incomplete generation sessions are
automatically deleted after 2 hours.
•
No Reuse: The face data is never reused for any other purpose and is not used for training,
analysis, or sharing without user action.
---
• No Biometric or Identification Use:
The technologies implemented in IRMO do not allow unique identification or authentication of a user.

[SECTION: Extra Information for Al Virtual Try On - GIGI]
Extra Information for Al Virtual Try On - GIGI
At GIGI, we prioritize the privacy and security of our users' data. We do not collect, store, or retain
sensitive face data or biometric information. The following outlines how we handle your photos and how
we protect your privacy:
--- [PAGE BREAK] ---
1. Face Data Retention
Explicit Statement on Face Data:
GIGI does not collect or store any face data or biometric information. Users upload their photos, which
include their face, to create virtual try-ons. These photos are processed using Al algorithms to apply
outfits to the images. After processing, the photos are stored temporarily until the user decides to
delete them.
---
2. Reasons for Storing Photos
The photos are used solely for the purpose of creating virtual try-ons. The images are temporarily stored
to facilitate the processing of the virtual outfits and to allow users to review their try-ons. Users can delete
their photos at any time.
---
3. Length of Time Photos Are Stored
Temporary Storage:
Photos are stored temporarily on our servers until the user decides to delete them. Once the user
deletes a photo, it is removed from our servers within one month. This retention period is due to caching
mechanisms that allow the app to handle data more efficiently. After one month, all data related to the
photos, including cached images, is deleted from our servers.
---
Reason for Retention Period:
The retention period allows us to clear cached data. Once the photo is deleted by the user, all related
data is deleted from both the app and our servers within one month, ensuring that no photos are stored
indefinitely.
---
4. Sharing Photos with Third Parties
No Face Data Shared:
GIGI does not share face data with third parties. However, user-uploaded photos are sent to third-party
APIs to apply the virtual outfits and visualizations. These third-party services process the images
temporarily to create the visualizations and return the processed data (outfit visualization) to GIGI.
---
5. Reasons for Sharing Photos with Third Parties
We share the uploaded photos with third-party APIs for the sole purpose of applying virtual outfits and
visualizations to the user's image. These third-party services are essential for processing the photos and
providing the virtual try-on experience.
---
6. Third-Party Storage of Photos
Do Third Parties Store Photos?
The third-party services we use do not retain the photos after processing. Once the outfit visualization
is applied, the data is returned to GIGI, and the photos are deleted from the third-party servers. No
--- [PAGE BREAK] ---
photos are stored by these services after the processing is complete.
---
Privacy Practices of Third Parties:
The third-party services we use adhere to strict privacy standards and do not store any face data. The
photos are processed temporarily, and once the virtual try-on process is complete, they are deleted.
These services do not retain any user photos for future use.
---
We value your privacy and work to protect your data. GIGI ensures that no sensitive data, including face
data, is collected or retained without your consent. Your photos are used solely for creating virtual try-ons,
and once you delete them, they are removed from both the app and our servers as described above...
