1. Information We Collect
1.1 Location Data
We use your device's location to provide accurate weather forecasts for your area.
• Purpose: To deliver location-specific weather forecasts.
•
Data Handling: Location data is not stored or shared with any third parties.
1.2 Account Information
We collect your email address if you register an account to manage shared images within the app.
•
Data Handling: Email addresses are not shared with any third parties.
1.3 Shared Images
Users may upload weather-related images, which are stored on our servers for app functionality.
•
Data Handling: Shared images are not shared with third parties and can be deleted or managed by
users anytime.
---
2. Purpose of Data Collection
• To improve our services and provide a better user experience.
•
To display personalized advertisements through:
• AdMob by Google
• Meta Audience Network
•
To analyze user behavior for app optimization.

4. User Rights
•
•
Access and Delete Data: You can view or delete shared images at any time through the app.
Withdraw Consent: You can opt out of personalized ads in the app settings.
• Control Data Collection: Disable location sharing in your device settings.
